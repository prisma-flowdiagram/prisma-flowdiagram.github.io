PRISMA2020 Interactive Demo - Other Study Identification

Ambulance clinicians' identification and management of male victims of intimate partner violence

A search using a search engine, google, will be run to obtain relevant grey literature. To reduce bias and aid in reproducibility, the search will be run from a university network, in incognito mode, with private results turned off, signed out search activity turned off, and verbatim mode turned on. Some large, known, journal sites will be excluded, as relevant journal articles will be located via the database search.

Additional studies will also be identified by reviewers either when they appear in the references section of eligible articles or where they have appeared in previous reviews and systematic reviews. These will be added to the review if they fit the eligibility criteria.
